Mini Mart Management System

Technology: C#

The Mini-Mart Management System is designed to streamline market operations by organizing personnel and stock into a structured hierarchy. At its core, Admin tracks employees; identified by unique Emp_IDs and other contact details which are categorized into Managers and Staff via "is-a" relationships. Admins hold high-level authority to manage the general employee table, while Managers are specifically tasked with overseeing Work schedules (shifts) and the central Inventory. To ensure operational efficiency, the Staff members are assigned to work shifts and are responsible for performing routine checks on the inventory. The inventory itself is organized into distinct categories; Grocery, Snacks and Fruits, each tracking essential data points including item numbers, names, pricing and quantities. This interconnected structure allows the MiniMart to maintain clear accountability between working bodies while ensuring precise monitoring of product levels across various points.

Velocity Run

Technology: C++

VELOCITY RUN is a 3D game of real time car race. It incorporates OpenGL and GLUT libraries for rendering interactive graphics. OpenGL is one of the most widely used graphic programming interfaces which can be used for both 2D and 3D graphics rendering and this game makes use of this along with the GLUT library. The topic falls under the domain of computer graphics. It demonstrates various aspects of computer graphics such as rendering of 3D models, transformations, lighting, animations, interactions with the user, detecting collision, and the effect of the environment. The game is created as an endless runner game, in which the player has to drive a car and avoid obstacles while picking up objects along the way. Some maps have diverse environments and play modes. The game employs sophisticated graphics such as fog effects, weather simulation, lighting transitions, terrain rendering, water effects, and head-up display (HUD) mechanisms for greater realism. The main goal of this project is applying theoretical concepts of computer graphics into engaging gaming application using C++ and OpenGL.

AIUB sports Management System

Technology: Figma

American International University-Bangladesh organizes many sports activities and tournaments every year. These sports events help students stay active and healthy. Sports also help students learn teamwork, discipline, and leadership. However, the current system for sports information is not very good. Most sports information is shared through Facebook posts or official notices. Because of this, many students miss important information. Students often do not know the match schedule, tournament registration date, or gym membership notice. Another problem is that students must go to the sports office to book sports slots or get information. This process takes time and is not easy for students. Sometimes there is also confusion about schedules and event updates. Because there is no central digital system, the information is not well organized. Students face problems getting information quickly. The sports department also finds it difficult to manage records and event data. For this reason, a digital system is needed. The Integrated Sports Information and Management System will help manage sports information in one place. Students and administrators will be able to use the system easily.
